Per Capita Income & Expense Analysis in the United States from 1980 - 2020

According to census.gov, per capita income is defined as the "mean income computed for every man, woman, and child in a particular group including those living in group quarters. It is derived by dividing the aggregate income of a particular group by the total population in that group. This measure is rounded to the nearest whole dollar."
